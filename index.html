Welcome to our School!
#

Our Institution, which is 65 years old, is one of the pioneer institutions of Ranjhi in the field of Education.

The great tradition of the past are maintained and improved upon as the years go by. The top priority of our school is the overall development of the pupil through various activities with special emphasis on moral values and academic excellence. Due importance to physical development through games and sports is also given.

Thus, in keeping with the motto of our school "Virtue Alone Ennobles", every year our institution turns out young citizens of character, balanced in mind , body and spirit-in-services of mankind and the nation.

#

Education must be a partnership between the school and parents if we
are to prepare our children well to meet the challenges and needs of the
future. Hence, we need your co-operation, dear parents, in this connection.

This website is liaison between the parents and the school. Any problem
regarding your ward, may be conveyed through this calendar or you may
seek an appointment with the Principal. Please co-operate with us and
instruct your child to abide by the regulations of the school.

May our Divine Guru fulfil our vision and aspirations for all the
student who pass through the portals of S.J.C. Ranjhi, Jabalpur.
